Web Development through Inspect

Welcome to web development through inspect! In this series, we will explore how webpages on the internet work by looking at the code for actual webpages using chrome's 'inspect' tool. We will be looking at simple webpages on the internet, such as plainvanillaweb.com, along with this website designed for learning web development using inspect. To get started, you can inspect this webpage (see video below on how to do this with a two-finger click or the menu) or click on intro.html and inspect that.
